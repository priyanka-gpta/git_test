Sambhar Rice

Description

Sambhar rice is also called Sambhar Sadam. There are multiple ways that sambhar can be made and it is prepared differently in different states in India.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil, 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 2 dried red chilli
  • few curry leaves, 6 shallots, halves
  • 1 potato, chopped, 1 carrot, chopped
  • 15 piece drumstick
  • 6 beans, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp salt, 1 cup tamarind extract, water, as required

Steps

  1. Firstly, in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil, 1 tbsp ghee. splutter 1 tsp mustard, 2 dried red chilli, pinch hing and few curry leaves.
  2. add 6 shallots and saute for a minute. also add 1 potato, 1 carrot, 15 piece drumstick, 6 beans and 1 tomato.
  3. further, add ½ tsp turmeric and 1 tsp salt. saute for a minute or until the vegetables turn aromatic
  4. now add 1½ cup water, cover and boil for 10 minutes or until the vegetables are cooked well.
  5. add 1 cup tamarind extract and boil for 3 minutes or until the raw flavour of tamarind goes away.
  6. once everything comes to a boil add in cooked dal.
  7. mix well and add water as required adjusting the consistency.
